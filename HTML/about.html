About Us

Slideshow Images

We don't have to try to be authentic because we already are. Our chef's have decades of combined experience preparing the best ramen in Tokyo and Fukuoka Japan. We take everything seriously, but you can relax and enjoy. We import ingredients and techniques from Japan so it feels like you're actually there. We even imported the craftsmen who designed and built our space. Please come visit us and enjoy the best Japan has to offer.
